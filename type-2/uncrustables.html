History

A long time in the cool year of 1995, the Uncrustable was invented by two dudes, David Geske and Len Kretchman. They started producing tons of the crustless sandwiches for schools in the Midwest. The Smucker's Company heard the buzz about this rad idea and bought the rights to it in 1999. Thus became the Uncrustables you know and love today.
